// THE MANIFESTO

The tools you use every day have become twisted.

Useful products are bloated, overengineered, and weirdly expensive. It feels like someone took perfectly straight utensils and bent them — just to charge you a premium to keep using them. Prices creep up, features you never asked for pile on, and leaving gets harder by design.

We prefer things straight. No bloat, no venture capital to feed, no lock-in. Just the core of each tool, at what it should actually cost — and if you'd rather run it yourself, the code is right there. That's the whole idea, shipped as working software.

// PRINCIPLES — the shared DNA

Same rules for every tool.

[01]

◆ No bloat

Just the core features you actually use. Nothing bolted on to justify a bigger price tag.

[02]

◆ Fair price, no lock-in

What the tool should cost — month to month. No mega annual prepay, cancel whenever you want.

[03]

◆ One account

Sign in once, use every tool. Your stuff and your login travel across the whole family.

[04]

◆ Bring your own AI

Every tool speaks native MCP, so your assistant can drive it out of the box — no plugins.

[05]

◆ Open source

MIT-licensed. Self-host it, fork it, or export and walk away any time. Your data stays yours.

[06]

◆ Honest by default

No dark patterns, no fake urgency, no "contact sales". The price is the price.

Your data is safe on secure European infrastructure — GDPR-compliant and private.
